Who we are

We are two 17-year-old students from Bus Pascal High School, specializing in Information Technology. Passionate about technology and its impact on the world, we’ve decided to take action on one of the biggest challenges of our time: electronic waste. Driven by a strong sense of responsibility toward the environment and digital sustainability, we’re working together to raise awareness, promote responsible tech use, and support initiatives that give electronics a second life.

Our mission

Our mission is simple: we want to make people more aware of the e-waste problem and how much it affects the planet.
